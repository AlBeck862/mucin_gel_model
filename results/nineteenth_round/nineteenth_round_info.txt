Lattice units: 10^-2 um scaling
Lattice parameters: 25 diffusivity subregions (linearly spaced, 0.1-1.25 um2/s)
Number of walks: 10
Time points (each particle): 5000
Random start locations: yes

histogram_1dt_dx: normalized histogram of displacements in the x-direction for 1*delta-t
histogram_10dt_dx: normalized histogram of displacements in the x-direction for 10*delta-t
histogram_100dt_dx: normalized histogram of displacements in the x-direction for 100*delta-t

histogram_1dt_dy: normalized histogram of displacements in the y-direction for 1*delta-t
histogram_10dt_dy: normalized histogram of displacements in the y-direction for 10*delta-t
histogram_100dt_dy: normalized histogram of displacements in the y-direction for 100*delta-t

msd_dt: squared displacement versus absolute time curves + MSD(dt) curve
msd_dtau: squared displacement versus lag time curves + time-averaged MSD(dtau) curve

step_sizes: folder of step-size plots for each particle
diffusivity_bar_plots: folder of bar plots of the frequency of each unique diffusivity encountered by each particle

Note: all histogram fits are Gaussian.
